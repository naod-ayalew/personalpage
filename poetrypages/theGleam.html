N.Y. The Gleam Pilots Aberdeen to Philadelphia Portrait Intérieur On High

Why pretend? Why disguise?
You are not more pleasing to Him
even if you cover the dirtiness. Take no shame
from being a little selfish. You see,

Everybody is swimming towards what he wants.
He cannot even see it from land, yet
there it is, the will to search it.

Who wants to remain on the beach asking the wind,
How? How? How?
You need this so.
Swim, or sink trying; it is worthy.
